Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Welcome to Fonkii ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, retain, and delete your information when you use our iOS keyboard application.

1. Information We Collect

Data Type Purpose Collection Method
Translation input text AI translation feature Collected when user taps the translate button
Subscription status, purchase confirmation Premium feature access verification Via Apple App Store and Adapty SDK
Anonymized usage data Service improvement Automatically collected during app use
iOS version, device model App compatibility Automatically collected on app launch

※ Translation text is sent to OpenAI's API for processing and is not stored on our servers.
※ General keystrokes you type are never collected or transmitted.

2. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period
Translation input text Deleted immediately after translation processing (not stored on our servers)
Subscription and purchase data Deleted upon subscription termination (subject to Apple/Adapty policies)
Anonymized usage data Up to 1 year from collection
Local data (favorites, settings) Retained on device until app is deleted by user

3. Third-Party Data Processing

Adapty (USA) Subscription management Until subscription ends
GIPHY (USA) GIF search functionality Immediately upon completion
Apple Inc. (USA) App Store payment processing Per Apple's policy

4. Keyboard Access

Fonkii requires Full Access to enable network-based features such as AI translation and GIF search. You can enable Full Access in Settings → General → Keyboard → Fonkii.

We do not collect or transmit the text you type in general use. The translation feature only processes text you explicitly submit by tapping the translate button.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of data collection

To exercise these rights, please contact us via email at the address below.

6. Children's Privacy

Fonkii is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will delete it immediately.
