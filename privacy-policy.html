Fonkii 🎀 Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Welcome to Fonkii ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS keyboard application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Translation data: Text you submit for AI translation is sent to OpenAI's API for processing. We do not store your translation history on our servers.
  • Subscription data: Purchase and subscription information is processed through Apple's App Store and Adapty SDK. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not store your payment details.
  • Usage data: We may collect anonymized data about how you use the app (e.g., which features are used most) to improve our service.
  • Device information: Basic device information such as iOS version and device model may be collected for app compatibility purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our keyboard service
  • To process your subscription and verify access to premium features
  • To perform AI-powered translation via OpenAI's API
  • To improve the app's features and user experience
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests

3. Third-Party Services

Fonkii uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: Used for AI translation features. Text submitted for translation is processed by OpenAI. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Adapty: Used for subscription management. Please review Adapty's Privacy Policy.
  • GIPHY: Used for GIF search functionality. Please review GIPHY's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store: Payments are handled by Apple. Please review Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Your favorites, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect any data transmitted through our services.

5. Keyboard Access

Fonkii is a keyboard extension. We want to be transparent: our keyboard does NOT require full access and does NOT collect or transmit anything you type. The translation feature only sends text you explicitly choose to translate by tapping the translate button.

6. Children's Privacy

Fonkii is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of any data collection

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
